Introduction


skills personal journey work

Hey there! I'm Abhishek Soni, a dedicated Computer Science Engineer who loves designing and engineering practical solutions to real-world problems.

My professional journey started with a summer internship at Paytm, and now I'm a thriving Backend Development Engineer at Bambu, a digital wealth management platform for financial institutions.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working in diverse roles, including Backend Engineer at Bambu and Niroggyan, Full-Stack Engineer at Webloon Studio, and Summer Intern at Paytm.

My passion for technology dates back to high school when, in 2014, I created an entertainment website for my friends and me to enjoy trending videos and Hollywood news. I'm incredibly grateful to have discovered my love for building tech products and designing practical systems early in life.

My soft skills include: fundamental thinking, brainstorming (I absolutely adore generating ideas) and active listening.

My technical skills include: Full stack development (primarily focused on backend with Node.js) and system and product design.
